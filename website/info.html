About

An Nguyen is an artist and designer who was born in Vietnam and raised in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. As an interdisciplinary artist, their works branch across a variety of media but meet at a common goal: to arouse a reflection of the human experience. Exploring subjects such as mental illness, death, and the supernatural, An's work draws inspiration and builds narratives from the macabre, strange, and occult.

An currently resides in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. They attend the University of Pittsburgh and are pursuing a BS in Digital Narrative & Interactive Design with an Online Media concentration, as well as a BA in Studio Arts.
